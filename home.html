ABOUT ME
I am a student working through the P-TECH program at Six Nations Polytechnic STEAM Academy, strengthening skills and abilities in software/hardware engineering & mechatronics.

When I was a young kid, I always had a deep fascination with computers and how they were able to captivate my attention in such interactive ways. I remember watching my parents play video games on their PlayStation 2 and sometimes being passed the controller to try out some simpler games. It wasn't until we got a Nintendo Wii where my infatuation with gaming began - as soon as I drove through my first race in Mario Kart I was hooked. At around age 9 I became more interested in how games like Mario Kart were made and eventually began learning game development through educational summer camps, Scratch programming at home, and eventually the Unity game engine. Now I am furthering my competency in and outside of game development, utilizing programming languages such as Java, C#, PHP, JavaScript, etc.
